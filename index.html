Nick Phillips


I’m a reporter at the Arizona Capitol Times and Yellow Sheet Report, writing about Arizona’s political world for our website, weekly paper and daily newsletter. I primarily cover the governor’s office, but my work also touches on statewide issues from water to housing.

Before moving to Phoenix, I worked at the Nogales International, writing about local government, migration, education, religion and more from a unique, Southern Arizona border town.

I’ve also filed freelance stories from Buenos Aires, writing about culture, the IMF and Argentina’s Museum of National Debt.

Some people have been nice enough to give me shiny plaques for the things I write! In both 2019 and 2020, I took 2nd place in the AZ Press Club’s Community Journalist of the Year contest. I also won best news story from the AZ Newspaper Association for 2019 piece about a rancher who feuded with the Border Patrol.
